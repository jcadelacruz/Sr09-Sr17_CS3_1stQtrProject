Cookies

Cookies just need taste. Hard cookies, soft cookies, they're all fine. No one wants to eat a rock or jelly when they want a cookie of course but generally, all cookies are fine. They're convenient to pack but you can eat them like chips. It's great. A lot of cookies won't be sticky to hold or anything. So cookies taste good and they pack well.

Breads

Breads are diverse because of the vast difference in texture. Hard and crunchy, crispy, or powdery. Soft and creamy, fluffy, or... powdery. They can be sugary, cheesy, savory, or all of the above (somehow). One can argue that this applies to all types of food, to which I say shh.

Packed snacks

I'm talking about snacks like bread stix and eggnog and loaded; snacks with a bunch of stuff in them. These are great, most of the time. You get to nom on them for a long time, just enjoying the act of eating.
