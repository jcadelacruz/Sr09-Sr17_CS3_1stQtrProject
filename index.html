Best Snacks In The Universe

Introduction

Snacks are practically essentials for the life of a pisay student. A good snack could make a bad day great when used properly. Of course, not all snacks are created equal. Some snacks are better than others. All in all, we need to find the best snacks to maximize enjoyment in pisay.

Since having one snack for the rest of highschool would be irrational, we will be ranking 20 snacks.

Mechanics of Ranking

Though this ranking will have a basis in fact(availability of and residue left by snack), it will also include our opinions(taste). As such, these three will be the dependent variables in our ranking:

  • availability
  • taste
  • residue
